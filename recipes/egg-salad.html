Egg Salad

Description

Egg salad is a classic, creamy cold dish consisting of chopped hard-boiled eggs bound together with a rich dressing made primarily from mayonnaise and seasoned with ingredients like mustard, salt, and pepper. It often includes other chopped elements for flavor and crunch, such as celery, onions, or fresh herbs like chives and dill, and is most famously served as the filling for a sandwich on bread or toast, though it is also enjoyed on crackers or over a bed of greens.

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water
  3. to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
  4. Remove eggs from hot water; cool, peel, and chop.
  5. Place chopped eggs in a bowl; stir in mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard. Season with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  6. Stir and serve on your favorite bread, crackers, or salad greens.
